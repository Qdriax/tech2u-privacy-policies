This privacy policy (the "Policy") describes how XSave Video & GIF Downloader ("we," "our," or "the App") handles your information. By installing and using the App, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

1 Information We Collect (and Do Not Collect)

Personal Data

No Personal Information Collected: We do not collect, request, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII), such as your name, email address, physical address, phone number, or contacts.

No Account Creation: You do not need to create an account or log in to use the App.

Downloaded Media and Content

Local Storage Only: Any video or GIF you download is saved directly to your device's local gallery/storage. We do not upload your downloaded media, search queries, or analyzed links to external servers. All processing and storage are strictly local to your device.

2 Permissions Required by the App

To function properly, the App requires the following standard Android system permissions:

android.permission.INTERNET

Required to analyze public X links, fetch media streams, download videos/GIFs, and load Google AdMob ads.

Storage Access / Media Store Permissions

Required to write and save downloaded media files directly into your device's public Gallery or Downloads folder.

4 Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal data directly, we do not store any children's PII.

5 Data Security

We are committed to securing your trust. Because all downloads and personalizations are processed entirely on your physical device, your media is as secure as your phone itself.

We encourage users to maintain standard device security measures (passcodes, biometric locks) to protect their downloaded content.
