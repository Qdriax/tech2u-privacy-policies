2 How We Use Information

Any information processed by the integrated third-party SDKs is used solely to:

  • Deliver, personalize, and optimize in-app advertising.

  • Maintain and improve user experience within the tournament predictor interface.

3 Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent any user information. We only share data collected through integrated third-party services (such as Google AdMob) for ad serving and operational analytics, as described in Section 1.

4 Data Retention and Deletion

All user inputs and sports brackets are kept exclusively on your local device. Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all locally stored choices and data.

To reset or limit your device's Advertising ID, you can do so at any time via your Android system settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Reset/Delete Advertising ID).

5 Children's Privacy

The App is designed for a general audience of sports fans aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal data, we will immediately take steps to ensure this data is removed from any associated third-party service logs.
