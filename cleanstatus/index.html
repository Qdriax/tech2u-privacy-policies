1 Information Collection and Use

Local Storage and Media Access

CleanStatus is a local utility application designed to help you view, download, and manage WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business photo and video statuses.

Media Files: To function properly, the Application requests interactive folder access via the Android Storage Access Framework (SAF). The Application only reads files from the designated status directory on your device.

No Server Uploads: All status saving, viewing, and deletion operations occur entirely locally on your device. We do not upload, transmit, or store your personal files, media, or directory structures on any external servers.

Third-Party Services and Analytics

To keep the Application free of charge, we display advertisements and collect standard crash statistics through third-party services. These third parties may collect certain information, including but not limited to your Device Advertising ID, IP address, and application crash details.

2 Permissions Required

To deliver the core functionality, the Application may request the following permissions:

android.permission.INTERNET

Required to load advertisements from Google AdMob and process optional premium ad-free purchases.

Storage Access Framework (SAF)

Requested interactively to allow the Application to read media statuses from your local WhatsApp directories safely and output them to your device's downloads folder.
