1 Introduction

Welcome to Senti MMF & T-Bills. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and the choices you have regarding your data when using our application.

2 Information We Collect

Data Minimization Guarantee: Senti MMF & T-Bills does not require user registration and does not collect personally identifiable information (PII) like your name, phone number, or physical address.

We only collect minimal technical telemetry data required for app operations, ad rendering via AdMob, and analytics, such as device identifiers and network state.

3 Permissions Requested

The application requests only the minimum permissions necessary to function:

android.permission.INTERNET

Allows the application to fetch the latest Money Market Funds and Treasury Bills rates from our dynamic repository, and to serve advertisements.

android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE

Allows the application to detect whether a connection is active and trigger offline cache fallback logic gracefully.
