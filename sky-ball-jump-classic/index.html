1 Introduction

We take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy describes how Sky Ball Jump Classic collects, uses, and shares information in connection with our mobile game application.

2 Information Collection & Permissions

The application is free and monetized through ads. We require permissions to access the network state and connect to the internet to load ads:

android.permission.INTERNET

Allows the app to connect to the internet to load ads and check UMP consent status.

android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE

Allows the app to check network connection status.

3 Third-Party Services (Advertising & Consent)

The app uses Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) and Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) SDK. These services may collect device identifiers, location data, or cookies for personalized ad serving:

Google AdMob & UMP

AdMob serves banner and interstitial ads. UMP provides compliant consent management for GDPR/CCPA preferences, customizable from settings.

4 Data Minimization & User Rights

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII) on our servers. You can withdraw or adjust your consent choices at any time by clicking "Consent Preferences" or "Privacy Settings" inside the Settings menu.
